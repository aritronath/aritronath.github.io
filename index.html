Welcome to my homepage!

I am a scientist with a background in cancer cell biology, genetics and bioinformatics based in Los Angeles, CA. Using single-cell and bulk sequencing data, I study the mechanisms of tumor evolution and drug resistance. I develop machine-learning approaches that integrate vast amounts of tumor "omic" data with clinical information to identify biomarkers and design optimal treatment strategies for individual cancers.
